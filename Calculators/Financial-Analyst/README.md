This program acts as a comprehensive financial analyst by calculating the cost price, unit cost, and profit/loss percentage from basic sales figures. 
It is designed to help small business owners understand their margins relative to their initial investment.
The logic follows these mathematical steps:
Cost Recovery: Determines the initial investment by adjusting the selling price by the gain/loss amount.
Margin Analysis: Calculates the percentage of profit or loss using the formula:
	Percentage = ((Profit/Loss)/Cost_Price)*100
Unit Breakdown: Provides the exact cost required to acquire or produce a single item.
