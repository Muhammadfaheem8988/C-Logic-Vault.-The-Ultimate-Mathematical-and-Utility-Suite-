This program calculates the total cost price and the individual cost per item based on the total selling price and the profit earned. It is a useful utility for basic retail or inventory accounting.The program logic follows these financial principles:Total Cost Price: Calculated by subtracting the total profit from the total selling price (CP = SP - Profit).Unit Cost: Derived by dividing the total cost price by the number of items (Unit/CP = (Total_CP)/(Quantity).The results provide a clear breakdown of the initial investment required for the goods sold.
